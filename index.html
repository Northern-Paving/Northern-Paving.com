Services


Paving and Seal Coating

Northern is equipped to handle all of your commercial, municipal or residential paving needs including roadways, driveways, and parking lots. We are equally suitable for repairs and maintenance or new construction:

  • New asphalt construction
  • Sealcoating and crackfilling
  • Asphalt patching and resurfacing
  • Stone parking lot or driveway installation
  • Stoneseal driveways
  • Parking lot maintenance

An application of sealcoating preserves the appearance of new asphalt. It also restores the like-new look of old asphalt that has become grey, brittle, porous, rough, and grime-filled. Oil spots and patches disappear leaving a fresh, black surface. These points illustrate the advantages of Sealcoating:

  • Reduces Maintenance Cost
  • Prevents gas, oil, and salt damage
  • Beautifies and renews pavement
  • Prevents weather damage and oxidation
  • Adds value to your property

A properly maintained pavement will last many years with only minor repairs. Traffic and parking lines last longer and require less-frequent, costly restriping. The need for extensive patching is reduced. Ultimately, protection now will mean savings later.

About Us

Northern Paving was started by Ray Caterino Sr. in 1977, with one truck and a small seal coating spreader. Ray and his crew began by paving driveways and parking lots, and quickly gained a reputation for hard work, integrity and a commitment to quality. Since that time, Northern has grown to become one of Cape Cod's largest Paving and Excavating Contractors for both residential and commercial customers.
